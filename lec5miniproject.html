Overview

blood donation is a voluntary procedure that can help saves lives.There are several typesof blood donation Each type helps meet different medical needs. This is needed for mollions of people every year, and process is used in surgeries or accidents or for some diseases that required blood transfusion.

whole blood donation

whole blood donation is the most common type of blood donation. During this donation,you donate about a pint(about half a liter)of whole blood. The blood is then separated into its components-red cells,plasma and sometimes platelets.

Apheresis

During apharasis,you are hooked up to a machine that collects and seperates different part of your body. In donor apheresis,a healthy person donates blood using the apheresis machine,which is programmed to collect the desired blood component-either red blood cells,white blood cells,platelets,or plasma. The component can be stored and distributed to hospitals ,to be given to a patient in need.
